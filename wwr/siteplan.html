Overview

Purpose

Hello! I'm Jonathan Pittella. I'm a web programmer at Infinite Software Solutions. My purpose is to help you plan and develop your web page. Even if you think you can't do it, I believe in you. Share your ideas for websites or mobile applications with our team, and together we will build your dream. Don't worry if you have no prior experience; we are here to support you. You decide how your site will be, and we will implement your vision. With just one hour per week, we can schedule meetings with you. Let us open up a new world of possibilities and make your dream come true with Infinite Software.

Audience

We welcome individuals of all ages who are brave and bold enough to pursue their dreams. Whether you are 14 years old or think you're too old, we want to work with you. If you have an idea or a project you want to execute, we want to help you become the great mind behind projects that can change lives. If you want to make a difference in your life and the lives of others around you, contact us.
